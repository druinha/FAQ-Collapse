Frequently Asked Questions

Where did I come from?

Humans first evolved in Africa, and much of human evolution occurred on that continent, so Africa

Why am I here?

Life's just an accident and so are you. You can find short term reasons for living like you're here because your parents wanted to have children, etc.

Where am I going?

Run where it's foul, run where it's fair No matter where I run, I meet myself there

Who am I?

Asking "Who am I?" implies a person can only be one, fixed thing – a belief which only causes more anxiety.

Do we have free will?

Newton's laws of physics simply don't allow for free will to exist – once a physical system is set in motion, it follows a completely predictable path.
